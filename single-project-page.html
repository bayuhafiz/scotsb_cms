Trident House - Centralized offices near the Glasgow Airport. 

Trident House occupies a prominent position on Renfrew Road overlooking the M8 Motorway. The property which lies approximately 8 miles west of Glasgow and within 1 mile of Glasgow Airport.  It is currently completely tenanted and is generating yield rates of over 10%.

Facilities include:
• Full raised access floors with floor boxes
• Suspended ceilings incorporating recessed lighting
• Double glazed windows throughout
• Male, female and disabled toilet facilities
• Disabled access throughout
• Ample parking spaces (1 to 220 sq ft)

There are plans to redevelop Trident House into a student accommodation to support the rapidly growing UWS campus in Glasgow.
