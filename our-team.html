Shazad Bakhsh

director - scotland

Mr. Shazad Bakhsh is Executive Director of Scotsbridge Holdings. He is responsible for business development and formulation of corporate strategy. Mr. Bakhsh is a self-made entrepreneur and serves as Director of Bakhsh Group, an integrated company with exposure in real estate, oil & gas, technology and education. Mr. Bakhsh has strong and established relationships across the UK, Europe, Middle East and Asia.

Mr. Bakhsh is also director of Etimon Ltd., a well-established Scottish based real estate management company with over 1,000 properties under management. Etimon is regulated by the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (RICS) and the Association of Residential Letting Agents (ARLA).

Kunal Nathani

director - singapore

Mr. Nathani is Executive Director of Scotsbridge Holdings. He is responsible for the formulation of the group’s strategies and structuring of transactions. In his prior role Mr. Nathani worked with Abacus Capital for 4 years within the group’s private equity and corporate advisory teams based in Singapore and Indonesia.

As part of his role at Abacus, Mr. Nathani has managed in excess of USD 200 million in transaction including the leveraged buyout of Southeast Asia’s largest self-storage business. Prior to his role in Abacus Capital, Kunal worked as an analyst at Citigroup Investment Banking in Singapore.

Mr. Nathani graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Business Management from the Singapore Management University.

kunal@scotsb.com

Neeraj Daryanani

director - singapore

Mr. Daryanani is Executive Director of Scotsbridge Holdings. He manages sales and business development for the group. Mr. Daryanani also serves as Director of Lachmi Enterprise, an established electronics distribution and real estate investment company based in Singapore.

Mr. Daryanani helped manage in excess of SGD 100 million of Lachmi Enterprise’s property portfolios and helped the group chart new investment opportunities into the Singapore industrial property space. Between 2010 and 2015, he led substantive investments into several developments including Oxley Bizhub at Ubi Road, Rivergate at River Valley, Midview City at Sin Ming, Paradise Island, Oceanfront, The Coast, and Berth by Cove respectively in Sentosa.

Mr. Daryanani was presented the 2015 Singapore Prestige Class Awards in recognition for his business excellence.
