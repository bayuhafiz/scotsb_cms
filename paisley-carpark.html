Long Term Carparks neighbouring to the Glasgow Airport.

Scotsbridge Holdings has acquired multiple assets adjacent to Glasgow's Airport. These assets will undergo development into short and long term airport carpark.

With the high demand for parking lots, expected increase in passenger flow and low supply of lots, airport car parking spaces are a reliable and promising commercial property investment.

The Sandyford carpark will be  located at Sandyford Road which overlooks the M8 highway and provides good traffic flow and visibility. Projected Occupancy for the carpark is 80% to 90% with high daily rental rates.
