Our Strategy

Property Acquisition and Development

Scotsbridge Holdings actively undertakes extensive commercial and residential site developments across Scotland. The Company’s key strength is underpinned by its ability to secure off-market transactions and strong local relationships with project managers, contractors and strategic partners.

Scotsbridge Holdings’ anchor development is the 0.7 acres Argyle street site straddling the Glasgow international financial services district (IFSD) and the Buchanan Street retail district. Scotsbridge Holdings is now in the midst of undertaking feasibility studies and seeking planning permission for the development of an iconic multi-tower mixed commercial development. 

The Company is also in the midst of bidding for additional anchor sites to deepen its development pipeline in the region.

Property Repositioning & Redevelopment

Scotsbridge Holdings aggressively identifies underperforming properties with scope for value enhancement through asset repositioning, change of use, securing anchor tenants and capital investment.

Physical and operational improvements are prioritized to reposition properties quickly, drive leasing, and achieve higher stabilized cash flows. Resources are deployed to generate favourable attention from the brokerage community and attract and retain tenants on the most favourable terms. Scotsbridge Holdings helps to create value and bring the property to its optimal performing level. 

Scotsbridge Holdings continues to explore partnership with international investors in relation to such projects and is currently undertaking two projects of such nature, namely, the enhancement of Trident House and change of use/repositioning of the Sauchiehall Street property.

Buy-To-Let Investment

Scotsbridge Holdings actively identifies and purchases portfolios of single family homes which are deemed to be sound investments. The primary advantage offered by Scotsbridge Holdings to its partner investors is the ability to pool purchases and acquire larger property portfolios. These sizable transactions typically result in greater negotiating power against sellers and deeper purchase discounts.

Scotsbridge Holdings actively shares these opportunities with partner investors allowing them the lowest cost of entry to participate in the Buy-To-Let Scheme which generates upwards of 7.5% yield per annum.

The Scotsbridge Holdings team alongside its key partner Etimon Ltd., actively evaluates tenancy risks, building condition and yield enhancement possibilities. Together with rigorous property management strategies and comprehensive networks, the Company aim for quick and effective turnarounds. Properties held by investors will be managed by our partner company, Etimon Ltd. This ensures continuity of relationships and long term partnership.

To date Scotsbridge Holdings has transacted 74 single family homes predominantly based in Glasgow. The Company is in the midst of undertaking the purchase of a further 129 units through various portfolio purchases.

So why Scotland, and why now?

When considering to invest, Scotsbridge Holdings examines areas through the lens of historical growth, employment data, population changes, supply and demand characteristics, rents, past and repeat sales figures.
