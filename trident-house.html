Trident House - Commercial and office building strategically located 1 mile from Glasgow Airport. 

Trident House is a prominent office and commercial building located in Paisley, within 1 mile of Glasgow Airport. The building occupies a prominent position on Renfrew Road overlooking the M8 Motorway. Trident House caters predominantly to businesses requiring office space in the vicinity of the industrial zones around the airport.

Trident House currently has in excess of 30 tenants across a vibrant mix industry groups including logistics, construction, energy, healthcare, education and engineering.

Facilities include:

  • Full raised access floors with floor boxes
  • Suspended ceilings incorporating recessed lighting
  • Double glazed windows
  • Localized environmental controls for heating and cooling
  • Disability access
  • 24 hour access
  • 2 passenger lifts
  • CCTV
  • Landscaped grounds
  • Ample parking spaces
