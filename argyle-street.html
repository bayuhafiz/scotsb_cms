Argyle Street - Iconic 2,800 sq m mixed used development in the heart of Glasgow City Centre.

Originally zoned for the 6-star Jumeirah Hotel in 2008, Argyle Street is one of Glasgow’s most prime development sites. The site is located in the heart of Glasgow’s International Financial Services District (IFSD) and offers one of the best business addresses in the city.

Argyle Street is located within 2 minutes of the Glasgow Central Station which allows business commuters access to London within just 4 hours.

Within 5 minutes of the site lie the iconic shopping thoroughfare of Buchanan Street and George Square. The site is also adjacent to the Radisson Blu Hotel and a host of local amenities including Marks and Spencer, Sainsbury’s and Tesco.

Scotsbridge Holdings seek to redevelop the site into a mixed hotel and office development and is undertaking feasibility studies on various developments plans.
